Kaal Soup

Description

Kaal Soup is a traditional herbal soup known for its detoxifying and nourishing properties. Made with a combination of medicinal herbs, vegetables, and spices, it is commonly consumed to strengthen immunity and aid digestion. Warm and comforting, Kaal Soup is a healthy choice for a light meal or as a restorative drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped kale or other leafy greens
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional herbs: parsley, coriander, or thyme
  • Optional: ½ teaspoon turmeric for extra health benefits

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and ginger, and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add chopped carrots and celery, cook for 3–5 minutes until slightly softened.
  3. Pour in the broth and bring it to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the chopped kale and optional turmeric or herbs. Simmer for another 5–7 minutes until greens are tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Use a hand blender to partially blend the soup for a creamy texture, or leave it chunky as desired.
  7. Serve warm, garnished with fresh parsley or coriander if desired.
